How it's built

Every roof is only as good as its worst layer.

Scroll to see what's actually holding your roof up — the parts nobody sees are the parts we won't skip.

01

Rafters

The timber skeleton. If this shifts, everything above it moves with it — so it's the first thing we check, not the last.

02

Roofing felt

The waterproof backstop. A tired or torn felt layer is behind more slow leaks than any cracked tile.

03

Battens

What every tile actually hangs from. Rotten or under-spec battens mean tiles that slip years before their time.

04

Tiles or slate

The layer everyone sees — and the one most companies quote on without checking the three underneath it.
